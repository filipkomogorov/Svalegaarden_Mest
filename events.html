Events

Svalegaarden Mest creates the surroundings for all of life's events. We want to turn your great events into memorable experiences with a focus on gastronomy and unique surroundings. Whether you are having a wedding party or a confirmation, Svalegaarden is most of the ideal choice. We are always open to dialogue about your wishes and we strive to provide you with the best solution suited uniquely for your event.

Wedding

If you are looking for a place for your wedding, Svalegaarden is the best choice for you. Our historic surrounding and our team of professionals will create an unforgettable experience for your special day. We can both assist you with the design of your wedding or you can leave it all to on us and focus on your special moment. Follow the button below to read more about our wedding menus.
