Events

Svalegaarden Mest creates the surroundings for all life's events. We want to turn your big events into memorable experiences with a focus on gastronomy and unique surroundings. Whether you are having a wedding party or a christening, Svalegaarden Mest is the ideal choice. We are always open to dialogue about your wishes, and we strive, together with you, to achieve the very best. We arrange parties for parties 20 people and up.
