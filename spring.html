Spring

Starters

Beetroot roe served with green asparagus, sour cream and fresh herbs

White asparagus with sauce Mousseline and fjord trout as well as lemon confit and herbs (daily price)

Steamed red tongue on summer spinach with mussels, crispy polenta and chervil

Main courses

Funen lamb fried in croutons served with parsley puree, parsley root, pickled lemons, and thyme. Add lamb cloud and potatoes
