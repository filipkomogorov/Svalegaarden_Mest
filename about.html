About us

In 1952 the beautiful merchants estate was rebuild at the corner of Thorsens All√© and Svalegaardsvej, and the estate was naturally named Svalegaarden. The idyllic estate still stands to this day at the same address and has set the frame for countless events and memories over the years.

In June 2016 new tenants came to Svalegaarden - Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest, bringing exciting new hope for the future.

Mette and Stefan bring new energy and passion- with a fresh perspective on the beautifully restored merchants estate, which up until then had only been a business for functions.

Svalegaarden today

Mette and Stefan have a vision to take the best from Restaurant Mest and continue it in the estate's beautiful setting in the heart of Aalborg. The thought behind Svalegaarden Most is gastronomy meets rural idyll, where the focus is on creating memorable experiences.

Svalegaarden Mest is wonderful to work at. The old buildings can be felt and they give us a natural humility and desire to bring the good experiences to our guests. We have found ourselves at ease in Hasseris' work with many different angles in gastronomy. Svalegaarden Most contains everything from private parties, meeting facilities, lectures to food out of the house. .
