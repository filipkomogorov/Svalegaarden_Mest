About us

Svalegaarden Most exudes soul and forms the perfect setting for life's events - from weddings, baptisms, confirmations, birthdays or just a cozy gathering. Our team of chefs and waiters is ready to welcome you with a smile and a high gastronomic level.

Our History

In 1952, the beautiful grocery farm was rebuilt on the corner of Thorsens Allé and Svalegårdsvej, and the farm was also renamed Svalegaarden. The idyllic farm always stands at the same address, and has set the stage for many events and experiences over the years. In June 2016, Svalegaarden got new tenants with a new spirit and bright hopes for the future - Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest. Mette and Stefan come with new strength and fresh eyes on the beautiful renovated grocery farm, which has so far functioned as a company business.

Svalegaarden today

Mette and Stefan have a vision to take the best from Restaurant Mest and continue it in the farm's beautiful setting in the heart of Aalborg. The thoughts behind Svalegaarden Most are gastronomy meets rural idyll, where the focus is on creating memorable experiences. The first years with Svalegaarden Mest Svalegaarden Mest is wonderful to work with. The old buildings are felt and give us a natural humility and desire to bring the good experiences to our guests. We have found ourselves at ease in Hasseris' work with many different angles in gastronomy. Svalegaarden Most contains everything from private parties, meeting facilities, lectures to food out of the house. .

Mad Med Mest - lunch arrangement for companies goes solo. Mad Med Mest is in a great development and more and more companies are joining the scheme. Therefore, in July 2018, we took a leap and moved this part of Svalegaarden Mest to a second and larger premises, so that Mad Med Mest can be allowed to unfold under the right conditions. Michel Lycoops is the helmsman of Mad Med Mest and has been employed as a chef since Restaurant Mest 'origins in 2010. We are very pleased that this collaboration sees a bright future for Mad Med Mest.
