About us

In 1952 the beautiful merchants estate was rebuild at the corner of Thorsens All√© and Svalegaardsvej, and the estate was naturally named Svalegaarden. The idyllic estate still stands to this day at the same address and has set the frame for countless events and memories over the years.

In June 2016 new tenants came to Svalegaarden, Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest, bringing exciting new hope for the future.

Mette and Stefan bring new energy and passion- with a fresh perspective on the beautiful restored merchants estate, which up until then had only been a business for functions.

Svalegaarden today

Mette and Stefan have a vision to take the best from Restaurant Mest and continue it in the farm's beautiful setting in the heart of Aalborg. The thoughts behind Svalegaarden Most are gastronomy meets rural idyll, where the focus is on creating memorable experiences.
